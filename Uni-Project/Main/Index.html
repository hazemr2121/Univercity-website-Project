Computer Science

It's the study of computers and computing as well as their theoretical and practical applications. Computer science applies the principles of mathematics, engineering, and logic to a plethora of functions, including algorithm formulation, software and hardware development, and artificial intelligence.

Information System

(IS) is a formal, sociotechnical, organizational system designed to collect, process, store, and distribute information...A computer information system is a system that is composed of people and computers that processes or interprets information.

Artificial Intelligence

(AI) is the ability of a computer or a robot controlled by a computer to do tasks that are usually done by humans because they require human intelligence and discernment.
